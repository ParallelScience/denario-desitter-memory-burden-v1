You must clarify if this transition naturally leads to a radiation-dominated Big Bang (reheating) or if it results in a singular state. The current plan assumes thermalization, but the mechanism for energy transfer from the graviton condensate to the Standard Model degrees of freedom is missing. You need to demonstrate that the energy density stored in the condensate is efficiently converted into a thermal bath, rather than simply vanishing or collapsing into a singularity.

The current results show that power-law functionals can "tune" the system to observed scales, but this risks being an exercise in curve-fitting. You must justify the physical origin of $F(M_{Pl}/H)$. Does the memory load $Q_{mem}$ have a microscopic derivation from the underlying quantum gravity theory (e.g., string theory or loop quantum gravity)? Without a derivation for $F$, the "selection" is merely a parameterization of ignorance.

The current results focus on the background evolution, but the *fluctuation* spectrum is what connects this to CMB data. If the memory burden feedback is too strong, it may suppress the scalar power spectrum or lead to a blue-tilted spectrum ($n_s > 1$), which is observationally excluded. You must prioritize the calculation of the power spectrum; if the model predicts $r$ or $n_s$ values inconsistent with Planck/BICEP data, the "dynamical selection" of $H$ is moot.
